The Team

Max Wibert

Max Wibert is a data scientist and a tutor in math and computer science for Varsity Tutors. With a rigorous training in higher theoretical mathematics from University of Pennsylvania and experience in multiple tech stacks including the python, node, and C++ ecosystems, he has a wide array of tools for critical problem solving and analysis. He has built projects on subjects including stable-isotope analysis, orbital mechanics, neuroscience, using tools such as machine learning, statistics, and homological algebra. He is an outgoing team player with enough get-up-and-go to hatch ideas for interesting, dynamic projects, and enough get-up-and-go to bring them to fruition.

Rob Creel

Rob Creel is a data analyst and math professor with strong proficiency in R, Python, and SQL. He has a strong personal drive and capacity to glean value-adding insights from tidy and untidy data. He employs his mathematics background and coding abilities to communicate compelling analyses. Rob has continued his academic achievement through data science work with Johns Hopkins and Northwestern University. An active contributor to various Chicago tech communities, Rob can often be found meeting with Chipy, CRUG, DevTogether, and FreeGeek. He recently completed a personal work project to organize classroom attendance data into useful summary information. With a bent toward lifelong learning and teaching, he demonstrates pedagogically sound habits in collaborating with and leading teams of varied backgrounds and skillsets.
